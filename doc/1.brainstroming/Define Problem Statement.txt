# Define Problem Statement

## Problem Statement

The real estate market generates a large amount of data related to property prices, locations, house sizes, number of bedrooms, bathrooms, and other important features. However, this data is often stored in spreadsheets or databases, making it difficult for buyers, sellers, and real estate professionals to analyze market trends and make informed decisions.

Traditional methods of analyzing housing data are time-consuming and do not provide interactive insights. Users may find it difficult to identify price patterns, compare properties, or understand how different factors influence house prices.

To overcome these challenges, there is a need for an interactive data visualization system that can present housing market information in a clear and meaningful way.

## Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is to develop an interactive dashboard using Tableau that analyzes housing market data. The dashboard will provide visualizations such as bar charts, line charts, maps, scatter plots, and filters to help users explore the dataset easily.

Users can analyze house prices based on various factors including location, number of bedrooms, bathrooms, living area, and other property features. The dashboard enables quick comparisons and supports better decision-making for buyers, sellers, investors, and real estate analysts.

## Objectives

• Analyze housing market data using Tableau.

• Identify trends and patterns in property sale prices.

• Compare house features that influence property values.

• Develop interactive dashboards for better visualization.

• Support data-driven decision making in the real estate sector.

## Expected Outcome

The project provides a user-friendly Tableau dashboard that transforms raw housing data into meaningful visual insights. It helps users understand market trends, identify valuable properties, and make informed decisions based on accurate data visualization.

## Conclusion

The proposed system simplifies housing market analysis by converting complex datasets into interactive dashboards. This project demonstrates the power of Tableau in creating effective visual analytics that improve understanding of housing market behavior.
